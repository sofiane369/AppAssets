Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers below. Can’t find what you need? Contact support.

General

How do I reset my password? ▾

Go to Settings → Security → Reset Password, then follow the instructions sent to your email.

Where can I download the latest app version? ▾

Visit the App Store or Google Play and search for “Acme App”. Updates appear automatically when available.

How do I contact support? ▾

Use the in-app “Help & Support” button or email us at support@example.com.

Billing

How do subscriptions work? ▾

Subscriptions renew automatically until canceled in your store account settings. You can manage or cancel anytime.

Do you offer refunds? ▾

Refunds are handled according to the App Store or Google Play policies and applicable law.
