1. Introduction

DZS Infinity LLC respects your privacy and protects your data under international standards. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store data when using MeeZen.

“MeeZen” is a trade name of DZS Infinity LLC.

3. Data We Collect

MeeZen collects only minimal data required for core functionality.

3.1. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device type and OS
  • Non-identifiable device identifiers
  • Crash logs
  • Aggregated analytics

3.2. Account Data (Google / Apple)

When logging in via Google or Apple (EMAIL & FULL_NAME scopes), we may receive:

  • Full name
  • Email address (or Apple “Hide My Email”)
  • Profile picture (Google only)
  • Unique user ID (UID)

We never receive:

  • Phone number
  • Passwords
  • Sensitive data
  • Any additional information without explicit consent

3.3. Age Verification & Eligibility

MeeZen is intended for users aged 13 and above.

MeeZen uses Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In exclusively for user authentication. MeeZen does not independently collect or request users’ age information during account creation.

Any age-related verification mechanisms, restrictions applicable to minors, and, where applicable, parental control features are provided and managed by the respective authentication platforms in accordance with their own policies and legal obligations.

MeeZen does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected, it will be deleted promptly.

3.4. User-entered Data

  • Intentions / goals
  • Custom sessions

Stored locally only on the device.

3.5. Analytics

MeeZen uses PostHog EU Cloud.

PostHog does NOT collect:

  • Personal identifiable data
  • User content
  • Sensitive information

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

MeeZen does NOT collect:

  • Medical or psychological data
  • Location
  • Microphone or camera data
  • Photos or files
  • Messages
  • Custom sessions
  • Advertising identifiers

5. Purpose of Processing

Data is used strictly for:

  • Account authentication
  • Improving the service
  • Diagnostics
  • Analytics
  • Subscription management

MeeZen does not sell or share data for advertising purposes.

6. Data Sharing

Shared only with:

  • PostHog (anonymous analytics)
  • App stores (billing)

Never shared with advertisers or ad networks.

We also use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and validate payment status without processing payments directly.

7. Data Storage

  • Local device storage (for user sessions)
  • Firebase Authentication
  • PostHog EU servers

8. Data Security

We implement:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure Firebase infrastructure
  • Anonymous analytics

No system is 100% immune.

9. Data Retention

Data is retained while the user's account is active. Users may request deletion.

10. GDPR Rights

Users may:

  • Access
  • Rectify
  • Delete
  • Object
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request a data export

Contact: support@meezen.app

11. Messaging & Notifications

11.1. Email

  • Transactional emails allowed without additional consent.
  • Marketing emails require explicit opt-in.

11.2. Push Notifications

  • Require OS permission.
  • Users can disable marketing push notifications anytime.

11.3. Local Notifications

  • Used for reminders only.
  • Not used for marketing.
  • No data leaves the device.

12. No Tracking & No Targeted Advertising

MeeZen does not perform tracking across apps or websites and does not use any advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA).

MeeZen does not use data for targeted advertising, retargeting, or behavioral profiling, nor does it integrate with ad networks such as Meta, Google Ads, or TikTok.

13. International Data Transfers

Some data may be stored in the United States through Firebase under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC).

14. Changes

Updates may occur. Continued use indicates acceptance.
