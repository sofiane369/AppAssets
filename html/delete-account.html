Delete Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the Settings section in the app when you are logged in.

To delete your account:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  2. Confirm your choice.

Your account and associated data (such as profile information and preferences) will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Need help?

If you experience any issues or would like to request deletion by email, you can contact us at support@example.com.

Data retention

Certain data (such as purchase receipts required by Google Play) may be retained for up to 30 days for legal or billing purposes before being permanently removed.
