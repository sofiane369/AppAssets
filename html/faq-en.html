Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers below. Can’t find what you need? Contact support.

General

Where can I download the latest app version? ▾

Visit the App Store or Google Play and search for “MeeZen”. Updates appear automatically when available.

How do I contact support? ▾

Use the in-app “Help & Support” button or email us at support@meezen.app.

Billing

How do subscriptions work? ▾

Subscriptions renew automatically until canceled in your store account settings. You can manage or cancel anytime.

Do you offer refunds? ▾

Refunds are handled according to the App Store or Google Play policies and applicable law.
