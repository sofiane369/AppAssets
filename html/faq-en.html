General

Where can I download the latest app version? ▾

Visit the App Store or Google Play and search for “MeeZen”. Updates appear automatically when available.

How do I contact support? ▾

Use the in-app “Help & Support” button or email us at support@meezen.app.

Why is private session recording limited to 2 minutes? ▾

Because impact comes from focus + repetition, not length.
Two minutes is a “concentrated dose” that helps you stay clear, consistent, and effective.

Why this works better:

One clear message lands faster
Repeating a single idea daily is more powerful than stacking many ideas once.
That’s why 2 minutes is perfect for 3–5 strong lines (e.g. 4 key phrases).

Ideal for affirmations & subliminals
Short, clear content reduces mental noise.
Instead of 20 lines, focus on 4 core lines you want to imprint consistently.

Easier to keep the habit
Short sessions = less resistance = more consistency.
Two minutes is enough for breathwork, presence, gratitude, intention, or a quick reset.

Great 2-minute session ideas:

  • Affirmations: 4 core statements
  • Subliminal: one focused theme + gentle repetition
  • Mini meditation: breathing + grounding
  • Gratitude: “3 things I’m grateful for today”

✅ Want it longer? Use the player modes:
Play For or Repeat

Example:
A 2-minute private session + Play For 20 minutes
→ MeeZen replays it automatically until it reaches 20 minutes.

Why can I add only up to 3 sessions to my Daily Routine? ▾

Because your Daily Routine is designed for consistency and focus — not overload.

Why 3 specifically?

  • To avoid overwhelm: more choices often mean less follow-through. Three is easy to stick to every day.
  • To go deep on one goal: it’s best to choose one intention or goal and reinforce it daily instead of splitting your energy.
  • 3 = the strongest stack for the same goal:
    1. Affirmations (conscious identity)
    2. Subliminal (subconscious repetition)
    3. Meditation / Breathwork / Gratitude (state regulation)

Example (one goal: confidence):

  • Confidence affirmations
  • Confidence subliminal
  • Calm meditation + gratitude

Want more than 3?
You can still play any session from your Library / Catalog anytime. The routine stays simple and stable because consistency is what creates real change.

Are my recorded sessions saved to my account or my device? ▾

In the current version, sessions you record are saved locally on the same device only.

  • If you log in on a different phone, those sessions won’t be available.
  • If you uninstall and reinstall the app, those sessions may disappear because they were stored on the device.

Session Player

What is Sleep Timer for? ▾

Sleep Timer stops playback after the number of minutes you set if the session is still playing.

Example (cuts before the end):
A 60-minute session + Sleep Timer = 20 minutes
→ Playback stops after 20 minutes, even if the session hasn’t finished.

✅ Best for: falling asleep without audio running all night.
⚠️ Note: Sleep Timer does not loop the session.

What is Play For for? ▾

Play For keeps the session playing for a set duration (in minutes). If the session ends before that time, it will restart automatically until the timer finishes.

Example:
A 3-minute session + Play For = 15 minutes
→ The session will replay as needed and run for about 15 minutes.

✅ Best for: extending short sessions without manually restarting.

What is Repeat for? ▾

Repeat replays the session a specific number of times.

Example:
Repeat = 9×
→ The session will play 9 times.

✅ Best for: repeating affirmations or subliminals a fixed number of times.

What happens if I enable more than one mode? ▾

MeeZen uses this simple priority order:
Sleep Timer > Play For > Repeat

  • If Sleep Timer is on: it stops everything when its timer ends.
  • If Sleep Timer is off: Play For runs until its minutes end.
  • If both are off: Repeat plays the selected number of times.

Example:
Repeat 10× + Play For 20 min + Sleep Timer 12 min
→ Playback stops after 12 minutes.

What is Listening Style for? ▾

Listening Style helps match sound intensity to your situation.
(Reminder: adjust your device volume first, then choose a Listening Style.)

  • Very Soft / Soft: ideal for sleep or discreet listening
  • Calm / Standard: for normal listening

✅ Important: Listening Style only changes loudness — it does not change duration.

Is Listening Style useful for subliminals? ▾

Yes — subliminals work best when they’re subtle and in the background, not loud or distracting.

Recommended setup:

  1. Set your device volume to a comfortable overall level.
  2. Choose a Listening Style:
    • Best for subliminals: Very Soft (or Soft)
  3. Keep the audio barely noticeable as background sound.

⚠️ Note: Listening Style only affects loudness, not duration or repeats.

What do Off and Unlimited mean? ▾
  • Off: the option is disabled.
  • Unlimited: playback continues with no limit (until you stop it).
